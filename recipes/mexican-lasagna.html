Mexican Lasagna

Photo by Franzi Meyer on Unsplash. Thank you!

Description

This is a classic! Mexican lasagna is a great meal for any family table. For this recipe, we use turkey, which could make for an interesting Thanksgiving meal as well!

It's also a quick recipe, and all of this (possibly including the oven cooking) should be able to be done in under 45 minutes for most people.

Ingredients

  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Orange Bell Pepper
  • 1 Med. Onion
  • 1 Pound Ground Turkey
  • 1/4 Cup Southwest Seasoning
  • Shredded Cheese (6 Containers)
  • Refried Beans (2 red)
  • Diced Chiles (2 small cans)
  • 2 tsp EV olive oil
  • 12 Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Dice peppers and onions.
  2. Heat olive oil in large skillet. Add diced peppers and onions. Cook until tender.
  3. Remove from skillet and set aside.
  4. Brown meat in skillet until almost done.
  5. Add chiles and finish cooking.
  6. Add onions and peppers to meat along with seasoning and 1/4 cup of water.
  7. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Line greased 9x13 pan with 6 tortillas.
  9. Spread with 1 red container of refried beans. Spread half of your meat mixture over the beans. Layer remaining tortillas. Repeat spread with other half of beans and meat mixture.
  10. Sprinkle 6 blue containers of cheese on top.
  11. Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
